Pokemon CAWPS

Play as a cop in a pokemon game.
completed english

Play as a cop in this amazing pokemon hack rom. Pokemon CAWPS has a very interesting storyline, some updated graphics, and a new feature set. At the end of the article you will find all the information of the game with its download.

Story

This the story of a young man or young woman who becomes a Police Officer in the Hoenn region. WARNING: There's a lot of violence, death and stuff described/depicted in this game. If you're uncomfortable with it that, than this game probably isn't for you

Features

  • Character: Play as a cop!
  • Starters: Get 2 starter Pokemon.
  • Gyms: No Gym Challenge or Pokemon League.
  • Trade evolutions: Trade evolutions are by moon stones. Eevee evolves into Umbreon with Moon Stone and Espeon wiht Sun stone.
