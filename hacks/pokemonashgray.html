Pokemon Ash Gray

Play pokemon anime in GBA format.
completed english

Pokemon Ash Gray is a fantastic Hack Rom that takes us on a journey to relive the story of the first season of the Pokemon anime, but with a twist. This time, the story is presented in GBA format, offering fans of the series a fresh new take on the beloved classic. Not only does Pokemon Ash Gray allow players to relive the story of Ash Ketchum, but it also includes new mechanics and features that make the game even more enjoyable. The second part of the game, Pokemon Orange Island, takes us on another adventure with Ash as he returns to his hometown and then heads to the Orange Islands.

Overall, Pokemon Ash Gray is a must-play for any fan of the Pokemon series. At the end of this article, you will find all the information you need to download and start playing this incredible game.

Story

Imagine waking up late on the day you're supposed to start your Pokemon adventure, and to make things worse, all of your rivals in the paddle town have already caught the coveted 3 Kanto starters. However, Professor Oak hasn't completely given up on you yet - he entrusts you with a rebellious Pikachu that refuses to obey anyone. Thus begins your journey in Pokemon Ash Gray, a captivating Hack Rom that faithfully follows the events of the first season of the anime. With over 116 events, you'll relive Ash's iconic adventure from obtaining all the gym badges to making it to the Pokemon League, facing various challenges and obstacles along the way, just like in the anime. And that's not all - the game also includes events from two of the movies in the Pokemon franchise.

Whether you're a die-hard fan of the anime or just looking for a fun and challenging Pokemon adventure, Pokemon Ash Gray is a must-play Hack Rom. Check out the download link and more information about the game at the end of this article.

Features

  • Team Rocket: Implemented Team Rocket's motto every time they appear in the game.
  • Squirtle and Bulbasaur: Removed their evolutions to make it more faithful to the anime.
  • Charmander and Pidgeotto: We have delayed their evolutions to make it not so tedious and also more faithful to the anime.
  • Graphics: Most of the tilesets are completely renewed.
  • MO: They will be replaceable with other mechanics such as the raft that replaces the MO Surf.
  • Pokemon available: All Pokemon up to the first generation.
