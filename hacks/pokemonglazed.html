Pokemon Glazed

Get ready for a time travel between worlds.

Pokemon Glazed is an absolute classic in the world of Hack Roms and one of the earliest ones I ever played, second only to Light Platinum (which was my first). The game has stood the test of time, not just because of its age, but because of how well-crafted it is. One of the most intriguing aspects of the game's story is that the player travels between two distinct worlds: the world of Pokemon and a world without them. This unique feature adds an extra layer of depth to the narrative, making it a fascinating journey for players. The game also includes Pokemon up to the 5th generation, as well as protagonists from other regions, making it an even more exciting adventure.

Overall, Pokemon Glazed is undoubtedly one of the best Hack Roms out there. Its inclusion of so many new features, paired with its intriguing storyline, make it a must-play for any fan of the Pokemon franchise. Story

The journey of Pokemon Glazed begins in the Tunod region, where we celebrate our 12th birthday and receive our first Pokemon to embark on our adventure. However, this is far from a peaceful journey through Tunod, as we are repeatedly transported to a universe where Pokemon do not exist and back again, thanks to a mysterious power. As if that weren't enough, an evil team is lurking around the ancient ruins of the Tunod region with nefarious intentions. But don't despair, because your journey will also lead you to encounter beloved characters from other regions who will assist you on your quest. Additionally, a Pikachu sporting a stylish scarf will serve as one of your rivals.

Overall, Pokemon Glazed offers a thrilling and unexpected journey filled with twists and turns, mysterious powers, and iconic characters. As you explore the Tunod region and beyond, you'll encounter danger, but also unexpected allies, making this an unforgettable adventure. Features

  • Region: New region called Tunod and Rankor with the possibility of going to Johto.
  • Starters: We will be able to choose between 5 instead of three
  • Pokemon Available: Pokemon up to the 5th generation with the possibility of capturing the initial Pokemon in the tall grass.
  • Rematches: Once we defeat the gym leaders and win the Pokemon league we will be able to do a rematch against them again.
  • Moves: Physical and special split.
  • Legendaries: All the legendaries from Kanto to Unova/Teselia.
