How to mega evolve in a Rom Hack

Mega Evolve your Pokémon and get ready for a Fight!
tutorial

In this section we will see how to mega evolve in any gba game in a few simple steps. This applies to PC and Android.

HOW TO MEGA EVOLVE IN ANY POKEMON ROM HACK FOR GBA/ANDROID


1. Get the mega bracelet in our game.

The mega bracelet is essential to activate the mega evolution in our game, as this is a general tutorial I can not give an exact location. The megabracelet is a key object, so the safest and normal thing is that it is not an important character in the game.

2. Get the mega stone of the Pokemon that we want to Mega Evolve.

Once we have the megapulsera and we can mega-evolve, but remember that not all Pokemon can do it and those who can do it need their megapiedra which is an object. Each Pokemon has its own Mega Stone and remember that only one Pokemon per battle can mega-evolve.

3. Activating the mega-evolution in combat

All right! We already have our key item the mega bracelet and our Pokemon with its respective mega stone, now what? It's very easy. The first thing we have to do in combat with the Pokemon with a mega stone already equipped, is to give it to fight.

Once we hit fight we can see that next to our life menu an icon appears, which indicates that we can mega evolve without any problem.

Then here comes the most important part, before using any movement or doing anything we will have to press the MENU button which is usually START..

If we look closely, once we press our MENU button the icon changes color slightly which indicates that everything is ready to be able to mega evolve.

Finally we will have to choose the move we want and that's it, we can mega-evolve and enjoy the most of our game of any Pokemon Hack Rom.
