Pokemon Advanced Adventure

Become the best pokemon trainer of all time.
completed english

Pokemon Advanced Adventures is a hidden gem among Pokemon Hack Roms. With a captivating story and innovative mechanics, it offers a unique gameplay experience. The game also features an upgraded graphics section that sets it apart from other GBA games, including Pokemon Fire Red. If you're looking for a fresh and exciting adventure in the Pokemon world, be sure to check out Pokemon Advanced Adventures. For more details, refer to the end of this article.

Story

You live in a city where you are constantly bullied for being from the poorest family. Despite this, you dream of becoming a Pokemon trainer. One morning, the city's Pokemon professor seeks you out. His granddaughter has taken over the Pokemon headquarters and her actions have caused chaos in the city. The Professor requests that you stop her and her 8 genetically modified Pokemon tyrants who have infiltrated the cities and taken over the headquarters.

As the hero of Pokemon Advanced Adventure, you must prove your worth as a trainer and face the challenges that lie ahead. The game features a renewed graphics section that sets it apart from other Pokemon Fire Red games, and its unique mechanics and interesting story make it a must-play for fans of the franchise. Discover more about this complete Pokemon Hack Rom for GBA at the end of the article.

Features

  • Region: New region with new story and custom tile sets.
  • Pokemon available: All Pokemon available and catchable up to the 5th generation.
  • Moves: New moves and updated to 5th generation and reusable MTs
  • Evolutions: Much easier to evolve some Pokemon and without exchange.
  • Graphics: New tilesets and new Pokemon sprites.
  • New Pokemon: 8 modified Pokemon that do not end up being fakemon but rather alternate forms of existing Pokemon.
