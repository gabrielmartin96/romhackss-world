Story

Pokemon GS Chronicles is an incredible remake of the popular second generation Pokemon games, Pokemon HeartGold and SoulSilver. What makes this game stand out from other remakes is its stunning graphics, which are even better than the original Nintendo DS games. Along with the improved visuals, the game also features new mechanics such as Mega Evolutions, which adds an exciting new layer to the gameplay. Despite being a remake, Pokemon GS Chronicles has its own unique story with exclusive events and characters that set it apart from the original games. The creator of the game has put a lot of effort into making it feel like a fresh and exciting experience, even for players who are familiar with the original games.

Currently, the game is only available in English, but a Spanish translation is in the works and will be released soon. Fans of the Pokemon franchise can expect a lot from Pokemon GS Chronicles, and the end of the article provides a download link to try it out for themselves. So, get ready to embark on a thrilling adventure through the Johto and Kanto regions with Pokemon GS Chronicles!

Features

  • Characters: New characters added to the story.
  • New scripts: We will have new scripts renewed in some maps.
  • NDS Graphics: Region mapped in HGSS style.
  • Mega Evolutions: Introduction of the megaevolutions in the main story.
  • Day and night system: With events based on them.
  • Music: Soundtrack based on HGSS with the best possible quality.
  • Physical/Special split: As from the 4th generation we will have the division of physical and Special.
  • Pokemon: Available up to 6th generation.
  • HM: New mechanics to replace the HM.
  • Moves: Moves invented by the creator and up to the 7th generation.
  • Pokemon types: Updated table of types.
  • New puzzles and mini games: Added new mini games and puzzles throughout the game.
  • Trainers: Most trainers with different Pokemon in their teams.
