Pokemon Clover

Get ready for an adventure as Professor Stump's assistant, filling the Pokédex and battling tough opponents and Gym Leaders.
completed english

Unlike many of the Pokemon Hack Roms we've covered on this blog, I have yet to play Pokemon Clover myself, but I've heard plenty about it. From what I gather, it's an old-school Hack Rom that's been around for a while now. One unique feature of Pokemon Clover is that it has over 386 fusions, which should keep things interesting. Although I cannot offer much personal insight, we are committed to providing our readers with access to complete Hack Roms, and Pokemon Clover is no exception. Below you will find a detailed overview of the game's story and features, as well as a download link.

Story

Pokemon Clover takes place in the region of Fochun, and it features over 386 new Fakemon of high quality that make the game unique and engaging. While the storyline is not the main focus of the game, the sheer number of Fakemon and references to the franchise are sure to keep players entertained and even laughing. This game stays true to the essence of Pokemon - capturing them all - while also adding its own twist to the formula.

Features

  • Regions: An adventure with two large-scale regions.
  • Pokemon available: In this case Fakemon and a total of 386.
  • Music: Fully customizable soundtrack.
  • Battles: Battle engine like the 7th generation.
  • Battle backgrounds: Fully customizable backgounds.
  • Pokeballs: New personalized Pokeballs.
  • Side quests: A lot of side quests around the two regions.
  • TM/HM: Reusable and in the case of HM removable.
