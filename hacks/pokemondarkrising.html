Pokemon Dark Rising

This is the beginning of a journey into many mysteries and powerful Pokémon that you can't wait to challenge.
completed english

This game has a very deep story, I played it a couple of years ago and the truth is that it is a game that I really liked and kept me entertained. It is complete and in English, it has 386 Pokemon available in the game up to the fifth generation and more new mechanics that you can see in the feature section. Be sure to read the story, I'm sure that when you do, you will be very intrigued and will want to play it. At the end of the article you will find the download link for the game.

Story

It all starts with a dream, a Pokemon suddenly appears and chooses you, not only to save you but to save the world. You wake up ignoring the dream and go out to your hometown known as Fells in the Core Region. Your best friend, who also had the same dream stops you at the entrance of your house to give you an emergency message from the town's Pokemon teacher. He asks you to look for a package from Professor Oak to deliver to him.

While undertaking this simple task you meet a girl who seems to know the meaning of the dream. After delivering the package you face a very strong legendary Pokemon and wake up unconscious in your house! You can help the professor, save the world and find out what the dream means.

Features

  • Pokemon available: 386 Pokemon from the first generation to the fifth generation.
  • Moves: Upgraded to the fifth generation and can learn egg moves per level.
  • Skills: Abilities from the dream world.
  • Heal on route: Chansey on routes to heal you.
  • Bosses: New mini bosses that you can face during the adventure.
