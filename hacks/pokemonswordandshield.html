Pokemon Sword and Shield GBA

Live the adventure of galar in Game Boy Advanced
completed english

Pokemon Sword and Shield for GBA aims to recreate the highly anticipated Galar region from Nintendo Switch, but in a Game Boy Advance format. The most impressive feature of this remarkable Hack Rom is the inclusion of the dinamax and gigamax functions, which have never been seen before in a GBA game. Additionally, there are several other mechanics that can be explored in detail in the features section below. This Hack Rom is available in Spanish and includes all features up to and including the Armor Island and the Snow of the Crown.

Story

In this game, our protagonist embarks on a journey through every nook and cranny of Galar, a region steeped in history and inspired by Great Britain. Their mission is to take on the challenge of acquiring all 8 badges and ultimately winning the title of champion by defeating the current champion, Lionel. The adventure begins with a choice of a companion from Grookey, Sobble, or Scorbunny.

What sets this adventure apart from others is the inclusion of a colossal mechanic - the dinamax phenomenon. With this power, players can overcome the various challenges that await them in Galar and even have the opportunity to face off against the Legendary Pokemon of the region. Don't hesitate any longer, start your journey now!

Features

  • Pokemon available: We will be able to capture Pokemon up to the 8th generation. .
  • Dynamax function: As well as the mega evolution, in this case we will have a dynamax system for GBA.
  • Galar Forms: Apart from the 8th generation Pokemon, their regional forms will also be available.
  • Wild Pokemon: Wild Pokemon system as in Pokemon Let's Go for Nintendo Switch.
