Story

The historic region of Ortbus was once home to a flourishing civilization that left behind a rich cultural legacy. Their devotion to their Gods and guardians is evident in the various monuments and temples they constructed. However, the passage of time and devastating earthquakes have left the region in ruins, with only their relics and history remaining as a reminder of their past glory.

Recently, seismic activity in the region has spiked, raising concerns for the safety of the remaining structures and the people living nearby. In light of this, Professor Redwood has reached out to new Pokemon trainers with a thirst for adventure and a talent for battling. The Professor's call to action is centered in the picturesque, rural town of Celanto, a seaside town shrouded in mystery and known for its Totem Poles.

As the protagonist of the story, you will be tasked with navigating the region and preventing it from being consumed by the land once again. With the help of the Professor and your rival, you will engage in thrilling battles, explore the region's fascinating history, and uncover the mysteries of Celanto.

The fate of the region lies in your hands. Will you rise to the challenge and become a hero, or will you succumb to the forces that threaten to engulf Ortbus once again? Join the adventure and find out for yourself.
