Pokemon Light Platinum

Travel through Zheri, with its varied weather and Pokémon from all of the first five generations.
Completed English

Pokemon Light Platinum is a classic if we are referring to Rom Hacks, particularly I have to say that it was the game with which I was introduced to the world of Rom Hacks and although it is true that it is already a bit old and the community knows it. To spare, let's remember that new people will always come to this world and that's why this game is here.


Although it was one of the first Pokemon games to come out, not only is it here for that, but it also has an incredible story and over time new generations have been added, new mechanics such as the mega evolution and much more. Below you will see its story and all the features that this great game has and at the end of the article you will see its download link.

Story

The main objective of the game is to become champion of Zhery obtaining all the badges of this region, then do the same with the second region, Lauren. In the story we will meet many of the protagonists of other games and even the anime such as Ash, for example, in our search for glory we will run into a villainous team called the Team Steam, we will have to help the protagonists while we leave doing with our main objective.

Features

  • Two new regions: In this game we will have 2 new regions, Zhery (The main region) and Lauren.
  • Two Pokemon Leagues: That's right, for each of the regions we have two Pokemon Leagues.
  • 4th and 5th Gen Pokemon: In Pokemon Light Platinum we will have the possibility of obtaining Pokemon of up to 5th generation (Although with future updates they may add more Pokemon).
  • New Sprite: We will have all the sprites for all the Pokemon and they will be seen in full body in combat (back-sprite) which is something that is rarely seen in Pokemon Hack Rom.
  • New maps: Being two regions we have new maps and this time huge with a lot to explore.
  • Safari Zone: A new and personalized safari zone.
  • World Championship: If the Pokemon league is not a challenge for you, you have an even more complicated one that is the world championship!
