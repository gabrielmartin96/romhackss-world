Pokemon Adventure Red Chapter

Experience the story of Pokemon manga.
completed english

As some may know, in the world of Pokemon there is a difference between the anime and the manga (something that usually happens very little in this type of product) and is that the story is completely different, just to say that Ash does not exist in the manga.

Clarifying the above, if you love Pokemon manga and of course the Hack Rom, Pokemon Adventure Red Chapter is the game more than indicated for you. It has many new and interesting mechanics but if something stands out this game is in its story that is a mirror of all the events that occurred in the Pokemon manga, whether you have read it or not you will have a good hours of entertainment with this Pokemon game. At the end of the article you will find the download link of the game.

Story

For Pokemon manga fans, this is the perfect game. Pokemon Adventure Red Chapter is based on the manga, not to be confused with the anime. The adventure of Red, Blue, Yellow and Green in a complete and unique GBA game. Not only for Kanto but also for all the regions, also knows the history of the leaders of iconic gyms as Brock and Misty but this time from the perspective of the Pokemon manga.

Features

  • Graphics: Updated and improved graphics.
  • Protagonists: Red, Blue, Yellow and Green as the main character of the game.
  • Outfit We will be able to change the appearance to our protagonist.
  • Day and Night: Day and night system.
  • Regions: We will be able to play in many regions of the original saga (Kanto, Jotho, Hoen, etc.).
  • Background: New battle backgrounds
  • Mechanics: Mega evolution in combat and Pokemon fusions.
