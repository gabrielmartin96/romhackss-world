Pokemon Mega Power

Discover all the mysteries behind mega-evolution
completed english

Pokemon Mega Power as its name suggests is a Pokemon Hack Rom with mega evolutions with a very interesting story. It is one of the longest Hack Roms with 3 regions and with Pokemon up to the seventh generation. At the end of the article you will find all the information of the game.

Story

Neil is a talented scientist from the Ivara region, he is trying to create a powerful Pokemon called Mewtwo. After a while, he succeeded. However, this is not enough for Neil, because he wants to make Mewtwo stronger. He wants to make it into a Pokemon that can mega-evolve without ordinary megastones but unfortunately he has no money to keep it.

Just when he thinks he has to resign, a man named Kasper appears. This man is a businessman, in fact, he is the leader of Team Delta, a mysterious organization in the Ivara region. He says he can help Neil finish his experiment. Neil has no choice, he joins Team Delta. However, he still wonders why he helped Kasper, and should he trust this man? Find out in Pokemon Mega Power.

Features

  • Regions: Ivara, Lande and sevii islands with new gyms in each of them.
  • Mega-evolution: All mega evolutions available.
  • Pokemon available: All Pokemon from Kanto to Hoenn and then a selection from 4th to 7th generation.
  • Graphics: Some new tilesets renewing all the mapping.
