Pokemon Dark Rising

This is the beginning of a journey into many mysteries and powerful Pokémon that you can't wait to challenge.
completed english

This game boasts a captivating and immersive storyline that kept me thoroughly entertained when I played it a couple of years ago. It's a complete game in English, with 386 Pokemon available up to the fifth generation, as well as new mechanics which you can learn about in the feature section. The story is particularly noteworthy, and I highly recommend that you take the time to read it, as it's sure to intrigue you and make you want to play. You can find the download link for the game at the end of the article.

Story

It all begins with a vivid dream where a Pokemon chooses you to be the savior of the world. Upon waking up, you shrug it off and head out to your hometown, Fells in the Core Region. However, your best friend, who also had the same dream, stops you at the entrance of your house with an urgent message from the town's Pokemon professor. He requests your help in delivering a package from Professor Oak.

During this seemingly simple task, you encounter a mysterious girl who appears to know the significance of the dream. As you complete the delivery, you find yourself facing a powerful legendary Pokemon, leading to you blacking out and waking up back in your house. Can you assist the professor, uncover the truth behind the dream, and ultimately save the world? Find out in this thrilling adventure.

Features

  • Pokemon available: 386 Pokemon from the first generation to the fifth generation.
  • Moves: Upgraded to the fifth generation and can learn egg moves per level.
  • Skills: Abilities from the dream world.
  • Heal on route: Chansey on routes to heal you.
  • Bosses: New mini bosses that you can face during the adventure.
