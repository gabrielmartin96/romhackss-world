Pokemon Eclat Pourpre GBA

The region is overwhelmed by a crime wave. An organization by the name of TEAM ROCKET carries out mass kidnappings of Pokémon.
completed english

Pokemon Eclat Pourpre or its translation to English, Pokemon Purple Shine is a game that I have personally played and I have to say that it is one of the most innovative I've ever seen in the world of romhacking, has mechanics never seen before such as the explorer kit to explore the subsoil of the region and to get valuable items or for example the replacement of flight by an object called Pidgeot in which we can use the times we want to travel throughout the region and many more things that you will see more detailed below.

Pokemon Eclato Pourpre is complete and in English, at the end of the article you can see the download link and a more detailed analysis in my Youtube channel.

Story

An organization called Team Rocket is carrying out mass kidnappings of Pokemon, creating unprecedented panic. The police are doing everything they can to put an end to this period of repression, but some trainers decide to take on Team Rocket on their own. Will you be able to put an end to Team Rocket's evil plans? Together with our rival and one more friend we will enter into an investigation about what is Team Rocket and what are their intentions, since at the beginning of the story, this criminal gang steals the Pokemon of our late father and we intend to find it at all costs.

In order to achieve this we will realize that overcoming the test of the 8 medals to then make us the title of champion of the region will also help us to recover the Pokemon of our father and to stop once and for all the Rocket Team.

Features

  • Map: A new mapping just like the story.
  • Secondary missions: Possibility of doing secondary missions totally optional and apart to the main story of the game.
  • Mechanics: Pokeradar available, Explorer Kit to explore the subsoils of the region, possibility to change the appearance (attire) to the main character, to be able to run inside the buildings and a lot of new mechanics.
  • Pokemon Shiny: Being able to choose the probability of Pokemon Shiny.
  • Moves: New moves, re-usable MT and division of physical attack and special attack.
  • Battles: Difficulty increases in the NPCs of the game and a new battle tower.
