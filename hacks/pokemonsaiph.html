Pokemon Saiph

Play this sequel to the Hoenn story like never before
completed english

Pokemon Saiph is one of my favorite Pokemon games, created by Vytron, it has a new and unique story and mapping as never seen in a Pokmon Hack Rom for GBA. This game is complete and one of its strongest points is to have two regions, and one of them is Hoenn, this added to more points in the features section below. Pokemon Saiph is already translated to spanish. At the end of the article you can see the download link and a complete guide on my YouTube channel.

Story

El juego tiene lugar algunos años después de los eventos de Pokemon Ranger Shadow of Almia. Después de que el cristal de las sombras se purifico, los científicos encontraron una manera de usar su energía como recurso. Una organización malvada tratara de robar el cristal para encontrar al padre perdido en Shenow dentro del cristal, y lo usara para dominar todo el mundo con Darkrai. Tendrás que detener el plan y devolver la paz al mundo entero.

Features

  • Region: A completely new region to explore, the Colen region.
  • Post-Game: A part of Hoenn in the postgame.
  • Music: A part of Hoenn in the postgame.
  • Graphics Style: Art style inspired by Pokemon Black and White.
  • Type: Fairy type included.
  • Moves: Infinite MT.
  • New Mechanics: Experience after capturing a Pokemon, new bag interface, day and night system and much more
  • Evil Team: Team Void.
