Pokemon Inclement Emerald

Play the hoenn story with increased difficulty.
completed english

Pokemon Inclement Emerald in short is the new Pokemon Radical Red but in the Hoenn region. For those who do not know what is this game mentioned above, what we mean is that this game is a definitive version of Pokemon Emerald, with endless mechanics up to the eighth generation. Mega evolutions, Dinamax, personalized Pokedex, all the updated moves, physical and special division and many more things. At the end of the article you will find all the information of the game.

Story

The game follows the same story as Pokemon Emerald but with an increased difficulty and some new events.

Features

  • Difficulty: Three modes, normal, hard and challenge.
  • Moves Physical and special split, all moves updated up to the eighth generation and reusable TM.
  • Events: New events that make that although the story is the same, it feels a little different.
  • Dinamax: Dinamax as in the eighth generation.
  • Mega evolutions: Mega evolution in combat and out of combat.
  • Pokedex: Totally renewed with many new data screens.
  • Mechanics: System of repellent as Pokemon Black and White 2, section of Ev's and Iv's, tutor of movements in all the Pokemon Centers, Dexnav and much more.
  • Rematch: Rematch against Gym leaders once we pass the Pokemon League.
  • Berries: A berry system that makes the game more entertaining.
  • Pokemon available: All up to the seventh generation and some of Galar.
  • Locations: New Pokemon locations.
