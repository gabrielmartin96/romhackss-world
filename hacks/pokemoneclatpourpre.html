Pokemon Eclat Pourpre GBA

The region is overwhelmed by a crime wave. An organization by the name of TEAM ROCKET carries out mass kidnappings of Pokémon.
completed english

Pokemon Eclat Pourpre, also known as Pokemon Brillo Purpura in Spanich, is a game that I've had the pleasure of playing myself, and I must say that it's one of the most innovative romhacks I've ever seen. It features never-before-seen mechanics, such as the Explorer Kit that allows players to explore the underground regions of the game to find valuable items, and the replacement of the Fly move with an object called Pidgeot that enables players to travel anywhere in the region at any time.

The game is fully complete and available in English, and at the end of this article, you'll find a download link as well as a more in-depth analysis of the game on my YouTube channel.

Story

An organization known as Team Rocket has caused a widespread panic by abducting Pokemon in large numbers. Although the police are trying to intervene, some trainers have decided to take matters into their own hands and confront Team Rocket themselves. Will you be able to put an end to Team Rocket's sinister plot? Along with your rival and another friend, you embark on an investigation to uncover the truth about Team Rocket and their intentions. The story begins with Team Rocket stealing your late father's Pokemon, and you are determined to recover it at all costs.

To do so, you must overcome the challenge of earning eight badges and becoming the champion of the region. As you progress, you'll discover that this journey not only helps you become stronger but also aids in recovering your father's stolen Pokemon and thwarting Team Rocket's plans once and for all.

Features

  • Map: A new mapping just like the story.
  • Secondary missions: Possibility of doing secondary missions totally optional and apart to the main story of the game.
  • Mechanics: Pokeradar available, Explorer Kit to explore the subsoils of the region, possibility to change the appearance (attire) to the main character, to be able to run inside the buildings and a lot of new mechanics.
  • Pokemon Shiny: Being able to choose the probability of Pokemon Shiny.
  • Moves: New moves, re-usable MT and division of physical attack and special attack.
  • Battles: Difficulty increases in the NPCs of the game and a new battle tower.
