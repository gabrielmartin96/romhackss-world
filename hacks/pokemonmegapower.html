Pokemon Mega Power

Discover all the mysteries behind mega-evolution

Pokemon Mega Power is a captivating Pokemon Hack Rom that features an intriguing storyline and introduces mega evolutions. It stands out among other Hack Roms due to its extensive gameplay, spanning across three regions and featuring Pokemon up to the seventh generation. If you're seeking a game that offers a unique experience with challenging battles, Pokemon Mega Power is the perfect choice. Make sure to check out the end of the article for additional information on the game.

Story

In Pokemon Mega Power, follow the story of Neil, a brilliant scientist from the Ivara region, who successfully creates Mewtwo but desires to make it even more powerful. His dream is to have Mewtwo mega-evolve without the need for megastones, but he lacks the funds to make it a reality. Enter Kasper, the leader of the mysterious organization Team Delta, who offers to fund Neil's research and help him complete his experiment.

As Neil joins Team Delta, he begins to question Kasper's motives and wonders if he can trust him. Explore the Ivara region and unravel the secrets of Team Delta while discovering new mega-evolutions and Pokemon up to the seventh generation. Pokemon Mega Power is not only one of the longest Hack Roms available but also offers a unique storyline and exciting gameplay. Find all the information you need about the game at the end of this article.

Features

  • Regions: Ivara, Lande and sevii islands with new gyms in each of them.
  • Mega-evolution: All mega evolutions available.
  • Pokemon available: All Pokemon from Kanto to Hoenn and then a selection from 4th to 7th generation.
  • Graphics: Some new tilesets renewing all the mapping.
