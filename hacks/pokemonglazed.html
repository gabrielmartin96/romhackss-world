Pokemon Glazed

Get ready for a time travel between worlds.
completed english

Pokemon Glazed is one of the first Hack Rom I played in my life, probably after Light Platinum (which was the first one) and it is a classic without any doubt, not only for how old it is but also for how good it is.

It has a peculiarity in its story and is that for some unknown reason, we travel between the Pokemon world and a world where there are no Pokemon, which I find quite intriguing and interesting, that added Pokemon up to 5th generation available plus protagonist from other regions and endless new things makes Pokemon Glazed one of the best Hack Rom that exist. At the end of the article you will find the download link and all the information about the game.

Story

It all starts in the Tunod region where we would be celebrating our 12th birthday, which means that we would get our first Pokemon and start our adventure, but if we were expecting a quiet journey through the Tunod region, we would be completely wrong. A mysterious power sends us to a universe where Pokemon doesn't exist and back again and again.

An evil team lurks around the ancient ruins of the Tunod region with an unknown purpose but evil intentions. But it's not all bad news, you will find in your adventure iconic characters from other regions that will help you and a Pikachu with a scarf that will be one of our rivals.

Features

  • Region: New region called Tunod and Rankor with the possibility of going to Johto.
  • Starters: We will be able to choose between 5 instead of three
  • Pokemon Available: Pokemon up to the 5th generation with the possibility of capturing the initial Pokemon in the tall grass.
  • Rematches: Once we defeat the gym leaders and win the Pokemon league we will be able to do a rematch against them again.
  • Moves: Physical and special split.
  • Legendaries: All the legendaries from Kanto to Unova/Teselia.
