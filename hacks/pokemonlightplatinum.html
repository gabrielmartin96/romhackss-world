Pokemon Light Platinum

Travel through Zheri, with its varied weather and Pokémon from all of the first five generations.

Pokemon Light Platinum is a timeless classic among Pokemon Rom Hacks, and for many including myself, it was the game that first introduced us to the world of Rom Hacks. Even though it has been around for a while, it remains a beloved and popular game among the community. And it's important to note that new players are constantly discovering this world, so this game is still very relevant. But Pokemon Light Platinum is not just any ordinary Rom Hack. It boasts an incredibly engaging storyline and has kept up with the times by incorporating new generations of Pokemon, as well as exciting mechanics such as mega evolution. These updates have breathed new life into this classic game, and have made it even more captivating than ever before.


In Pokemon Light Platinum, players are transported to a new region called Zhery, where they are tasked with collecting gym badges and becoming the region's champion. Along the way, players will encounter a host of new and interesting characters, and will uncover a mystery that threatens the very existence of the region. With epic battles, surprising twists and turns, and plenty of opportunities for adventure, Pokemon Light Platinum is a game that truly has it all. So, whether you're a seasoned veteran of the Rom Hack world or a newcomer looking to experience something new, Pokemon Light Platinum is a game that you simply cannot miss. With its engaging storyline, exciting gameplay, and vast world to explore, this is a game that will keep you entertained for hours on end. So why wait? Download Pokemon Light Platinum now and begin your journey to become a true Pokemon Master!

Story

The ultimate goal in the game is to achieve the status of champion in the region of Zhery, which requires obtaining all of the badges available in this region. However, the journey to become a champion is not without its obstacles. Along the way, players will encounter various characters from both other games and the anime, such as the iconic protagonist Ash.

As if the challenge of becoming a champion wasn't enough, players must also contend with the villainous Team Steam, who are causing chaos throughout the region. While striving for greatness, players must also assist the other characters in the game in their fight against Team Steam, all while keeping their focus on their ultimate goal of becoming a champion.

Features

  • Two new regions: In this game we will have 2 new regions, Zhery (The main region) and Lauren.
  • Two Pokemon Leagues: That's right, for each of the regions we have two Pokemon Leagues.
  • 4th and 5th Gen Pokemon: In Pokemon Light Platinum we will have the possibility of obtaining Pokemon of up to 5th generation (Although with future updates they may add more Pokemon).
  • New Sprite: We will have all the sprites for all the Pokemon and they will be seen in full body in combat (back-sprite) which is something that is rarely seen in Pokemon Hack Rom.
  • New maps: Being two regions we have new maps and this time huge with a lot to explore.
  • Safari Zone: A new and personalized safari zone.
  • World Championship: If the Pokemon league is not a challenge for you, you have an even more complicated one that is the world championship!
