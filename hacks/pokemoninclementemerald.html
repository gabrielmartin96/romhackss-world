Story

Pokemon Inclement Emerald for Game Boy Advance follows the journey of a young trainer from the Hoenn region. The player character sets out on a mission to become the champion of the Pokemon League, battling gym leaders and other trainers along the way. The game's storyline centers around the ancient conflict between two powerful Pokemon, Groudon and Kyogre. The two legendary creatures are said to have fought for control of the land and sea, respectively, and their battles caused cataclysmic weather patterns that threatened to destroy the region.

The player character becomes embroiled in the conflict when the villainous Team Aqua and Team Magma, who are respectively interested in expanding the sea or the land, attempt to awaken the sleeping legendary Pokemon in order to achieve their goals. Throughout the game, the player must collect gym badges by defeating gym leaders, battle other trainers, catch and train various Pokemon, and stop the villainous teams from carrying out their plans. In the end, the player must confront and capture Groudon or Kyogre to prevent the region from being destroyed.

In addition to the main storyline, the game features various side quests, such as the Battle Frontier, where the player can take on various battle challenges and earn prizes. It also introduces new gameplay mechanics, such as the ability to have two-on-two battles and the Battle Tower, where players can battle other trainers with pre-determined Pokemon.
